US election 2020: Analysis

Control of the Presidency, Senate & House

Republicans had control of the Presidency, Senate & House when Trump was first elected, before the Democrats took back the House at the 2018 midterms.

The last time the Democrats had a clean sweep of Presidency & Congress was between 2009 and 2011, during Obama's first term.
